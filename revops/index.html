Best Resources for RevOps Professionals in 2026

Revenue operations has gone from a niche specialization to a critical function at most B2B companies. The role sits at the center of sales, marketing, and customer success, owning the systems, data, and processes that drive revenue.

This is our curated list of the resources that RevOps practitioners actually use and recommend. No vendor-sponsored rankings. Just the newsletters, communities, and tools worth your time.

Newsletters

1. RevOps Co-op Newsletter

Newsletter from the 15K+ member Slack community with tips on revenue processes, CRM management, and career advancement.

2. RevOps Impact Newsletter

Jeff Ignacio's Substack sharing experiences building revenue operations at growth-stage companies.

3. Revenue Operations Alliance Newsletter

Monthly newsletter with news, innovations, and updates for the RevOps community.

Blogs & Websites

1. LeanData Blog

Blog covering AI in RevOps, orchestration, and go-to-market predictions. Hosts OpsStars sessions.

2. Clari Blog

Revenue intelligence blog covering sales forecasting, pipeline management, and RevOps planning.

3. The CRO Club

Blog and resource hub with RevOps newsletters roundups, podcast guides, and revenue operations career content.

4. RevOps Report OUR PICK

Independent career intelligence for RevOps professionals. Salary data, tool reviews, and job market analysis.

Communities

1. RevOps Co-op

15K+ member Slack community with peer mentoring, local chapters, and the RevOps AF conference.

2. RevGenius

Community of sales, marketing, RevOps, and CS professionals building the future of revenue through community-led learning.

3. Pavilion

5,000+ executive members across SaaS/AI/fintech with Pavilion University courses and Slack community.

4. Wizard of Ops (WizOps)

Slack community with 300+ companies for RevOps, SalesOps, and MarketingOps. Includes events and job board.

5. Revenue Operations Alliance

Community focused on growing and shaping the RevOps profession with certifications and events.

Podcasts

1. RevOps FM

Weekly podcast by Justin Norris challenging conventional wisdom on building predictable revenue at scale.

2. RevOps Podcast (by Revenue.io)

Howard Brown and Alastair Woolcock exploring insights, technologies, and strategies behind revenue science.

Courses & Training

1. Revenue Operations Certified: Core

Accredited certification covering the full spectrum of RevOps from data insights to team unification.

2. Winning by Design Revenue Architecture

12-hour live course on SPICED, Bowtie, and Revenue Architecture frameworks. $1,500-$2,500/person.

3. Pavilion University Intro to RevOps

RevOps foundations course available through Pavilion membership.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
