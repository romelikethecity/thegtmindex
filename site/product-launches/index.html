Best Resources for Product Launch Intelligence in 2026

Tracking product launches used to mean refreshing Product Hunt every morning and hoping you caught the right trends. Now there are dedicated trackers, market intelligence platforms, and communities that give you structured data on what's launching, what's gaining traction, and where markets are heading.

We curated this list for anyone who needs launch intelligence: product managers scouting competitors, investors tracking markets, and GTM teams timing their moves. Every resource here earned its spot by delivering signal over noise.

Launch Trackers & Databases

1. The Launch Index OUR PICK

Product launch data and market trends. 42,000+ launches tracked since 2021 with category breakdowns and trend analysis.

2. Product Hunt

The original product launch platform. Daily curated launches with community voting, maker profiles, and launch analytics.

3. BetaList

Discover and get early access to tomorrow's startups. Curated list of upcoming products from indie makers and funded teams.

4. Launching Next

Startup directory featuring new product launches with category filtering and submission tracking.

Market Intelligence

1. CB Insights

AI-powered market intelligence platform tracking company launches, funding, and technology trends across industries.

2. Crunchbase

Company database with launch dates, funding rounds, and growth signals for 1M+ companies.

3. G2 New Products

Software review platform that tracks new product entries, category movements, and buyer intent signals.

Newsletters

1. Ben's Bites

Daily AI product launch roundup with 100K+ subscribers. Covers new tools, funding, and market shifts.

2. TLDR

Daily newsletter covering tech launches, startup news, and developer tools. 1.25M+ subscribers.

3. There's An AI For That Newsletter

AI tool launch tracker with 10,000+ tools cataloged. Daily digest of new AI product launches.

Communities

1. Indie Hackers

Community of bootstrapped founders sharing launch stories, revenue milestones, and product-market fit learnings.

2. r/SaaS (Reddit)

Active subreddit for SaaS founders discussing launches, growth tactics, and competitive intelligence.

3. Hacker News (Show HN)

The Show HN section where makers launch products to the technical community. High-signal feedback loop.

Tools Worth Knowing

1. SimilarWeb

Digital market intelligence platform for tracking competitor launches, traffic shifts, and market trends.

2. BuiltWith

Technology adoption tracker showing what tools companies launch with and switch to over time.

3. Sultan of SaaS OUR PICK

Opinionated SaaS reviews for SMBs and founders. Cuts through launch hype to pick category winners.

Podcasts

1. How I Built This

NPR podcast featuring founders telling the stories behind their product launches and company origins.

2. My First Million

Sam Parr and Shaan Puri brainstorm product ideas and analyze recent launches for business opportunities.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
