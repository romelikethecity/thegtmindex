Best AI Search Optimization Tools and Resources in 2026 (GEO & AEO)

By Rome Thorndike

Three years ago, ranking on Google was the goal. Now buyers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google's AI Overviews — and the page that wins the citation wins the customer. The category has names already (Generative Engine Optimization, Answer Engine Optimization, LLM Optimization), but the tools and content are scattered across a few new startups and a handful of practitioner newsletters.

This is the curation we wish existed. Tools brands are actually paying for. Newsletters that publish original research, not recycled takes. Communities where practitioners trade what is working this week. Updated quarterly as the category settles.

Newsletters

1. SEOFOMO (by Aleyda Solis)

Weekly SEO and AI search newsletter from Aleyda Solis with 40,000+ subscribers. Covers Google AI Overviews, LLM citations, and ranking shifts.

2. Growth Memo (by Kevin Indig)

Weekly research-led newsletter on growth and search with 25,000+ subscribers. Original studies on AI Overview usability, AI Mode, and LLM ranking factors.

3. iPullRank Newsletter

Mike King's research-led newsletter covering technical SEO, LLM optimization, and AI search architecture.

4. Search Engine Land Daily

Daily news brief from the largest search trade publication. AI search is a recurring beat.

5. AI News You Can Use (by Marie Haynes)

Biweekly AI and search newsletter from long-running quality-focused SEO researcher Marie Haynes.

Blogs & Websites

1. iPullRank

Mike King's agency blog with technical research on how LLMs select and cite sources.

2. Aleyda Solis

Independent international SEO consultant. Consistently early on AI search shifts and Google ranking changes.

3. Search Engine Land: Google AI Overviews Guide

Trade publication of record. Maintained guide covering what AI Overviews are, how they're triggered, and the SEO implications.

4. Ahrefs Blog

SEO tool blog with original research, including studies on how content gets cited in AI Overviews.

5. Conductor Academy

Enterprise SEO platform's free content hub, with dedicated AEO and AI search visibility resources.

Communities

1. Online Geniuses

Large Slack community for SEOs and digital marketers. Active channels for AI search, LLM ranking, and tool discussions.

2. Traffic Think Tank

Paid SEO community with deep AI search threads and case-study sharing among senior practitioners.

3. r/SEO (Reddit)

Largest open SEO community. Useful for tracking what the broader practitioner population is observing.

4. Marketing AI Institute

Community and content hub for marketers adopting AI tools, with regular sessions on AI search and brand mentions.

Tools Worth Knowing

AI search monitoring tool for tracking brand and competitor visibility across major LLM-powered search engines.

Generative engine optimization platform for monitoring share of voice in AI answer engines.

AI search analytics platform for tracking brand mentions and citations across LLMs.

Brand visibility platform for ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews with optimization recommendations.

Tracks brand sentiment and visibility within generative AI answer engines.

Incumbent SEO platform's AI visibility suite. Tracks prompt-level brand presence and AI market share against competitors.

Ahrefs' AI search feature tracking brand mentions, citations, and sentiment across AI chatbots at scale.

How We Curated This List
