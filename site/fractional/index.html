Best Resources for Fractional Executives in 2026

The fractional executive market has grown significantly since 2020. More companies (especially PE-backed and Series A/B startups) are bringing in fractional C-suite talent instead of hiring full-time. But the ecosystem is still fragmented. Job boards are scattered, communities are siloed by function, and there's no single place to find what you need.

This list covers the best resources across all fractional C-suite roles. Whether you're a fractional CFO, CMO, CTO, or CRO, these are the newsletters, communities, and platforms worth knowing about.

Newsletters

1. fractional.news

Bi-weekly digest covering all matters related to fractional work and fractional executives. Tailorable to interests.

2. Fractional Executive Connection Newsletter

Monthly newsletter with AI tools, industry news, social media content, and recommended books for fractional execs.

Blogs & Websites

1. GigX

Fractional CxO directory for finding and hiring fractional executive leadership across all C-suite roles.

2. Fractional Jobs

Dedicated job board for fractional work founded by Taylor Crane. Posts fractional CTO, CFO, CMO roles.

3. Fractional Pulse OUR PICK

Job board and market intelligence platform for fractional executives across all C-suite roles.

Communities

1. Fractionals United

100% free membership community founded by Karina Mikhli. Thousands of current and aspiring fractional leaders.

2. Fractional Executive Connection

Marketing-driven community that promotes members to founders, business owners, and chambers of commerce.

Tools Worth Knowing

1. Bolster

Executive talent marketplace matching experienced C-level leaders with companies for fractional, interim, or advisory roles.

2. Toptal

Premium marketplace vetting the top 3% of freelance professionals. Expanded into fractional CFOs and executive talent.

3. Catalant

Vetted network of 100,000+ independent consultants averaging 21 years experience.

4. Go Fractional

Platform matching companies with fractional executives within 48 hours. Focused on specific background fit.

5. CRO Report OUR PICK

Career intelligence and compensation data for revenue leaders, including fractional CRO roles.

Podcasts

1. The CRO Spotlight Podcast

Weekly podcast by Warren Zenna (CRO Collective) featuring CROs, revenue leaders, and CEOs.

2. Fractional CMO Show (by CMOx)

Hosted by Casey Stanton covering how to attract, convert, and serve fractional CMO clients.

3. Six-Figure Secrets of Fractional Experts

Candid conversations with successful fractional executives about building independent consulting businesses.

Courses & Training

1. CMOx Fractional CMO Training

Training program teaching how to build and run a fractional CMO practice.

2. Fractional Executive Conference

Annual virtual conference with recordings, bonuses, and networking for fractional leaders.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
