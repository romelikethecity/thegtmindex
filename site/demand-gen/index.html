Best Resources for Demand Gen Professionals in 2026

Demand generation sits at the intersection of marketing strategy and pipeline creation. The best demand gen professionals are part strategist, part technologist, part analyst.

This list covers the resources that demand gen practitioners actually use. The newsletters they read on Monday morning, the communities where they pressure-test campaign ideas, and the tools that drive pipeline.

Newsletters

1. The B2B Playbook Newsletter

Weekly demand gen content applying the 5 BEs Framework to real B2B problems.

Blogs & Websites

1. Demand Gen Report

Leading B2B demand gen publication with news, research reports, and the B2BMX conference series.

2. Cognism B2B Marketing Blog

B2B marketing predictions, trend reports, and demand gen strategy guides.

3. Demand Gen Insider OUR PICK

Demand generation blogs, ABM tools, and community resources for pipeline builders.

Communities

Podcasts

200+ episode podcast with the 5 BEs Framework for demand generation.

B2B marketing podcast from Exit Five founder. Interviews with top marketers on demand gen tactics.

4. Breaking B2B Podcast

Hundreds of episodes covering demand generation, SEO, inbound/outbound strategy, and pipeline metrics.

Courses & Training

1. Metadata.io Demand Masterclasses

Frameworks, tactics, and ideas from customers and demand community members.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
